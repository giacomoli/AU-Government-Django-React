YOUR RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES
& METHODS OF PAYMENT
WRONG ENTITY
If this invoice has been issued to the wrong entity, please let us know as soon as is practical, but no later than 7 days after receipt of this invoice. If we have not been notified that this invoice has been issued to the wrong entity within that time, we will assume that the invoice has been issued to the correct entity, and any further action that may be required for us to recover the amount payable will be directed towards that entity.
UNABLE TO PAY
If you are unable to pay some or all of the amount payable due to financial hardship or cash-flow restrictions, please let us know as soon as is practical, but no later than 7 days after receipt of this invoice. We will consider your circumstances and may be prepared to reschedule the timeframe on which you are required to settle this invoice.
NOT SATISFIED
f you have not been satisfied with the service you have received, please let us know as soon as is practical, but no later than 7 days after receipt of this invoice.
DO NOT AGREE WITH FEES
If you do not agree with the extent of fees or disbursements claimed in this invoice, please let us know as soon as is practical, but no later than 7 days after receipt of this invoice. If we have not been notified that the amounts claimed in this invoice are incorrect, we will assume that they are correct and will seek recovery of the full amount without further query.
WITHDRAWAL FROM TRUST ACCOUNT
If we hold funds on your behalf in our trust account we propose to withdraw as much of these funds as necessary to pay any or all of this invoice after 14 days. If you object to this proposed withdrawal, please let us know as soon as is practical but no later than 14 days after receipt of this invoice.
STANDARD RECOVERY PROCEDURES
In accordance with the terms of our engagement, you are required to settle this account within 14 days of its receipt. If we have not received payment by that date, we will begin our standard recovery procedures. You will be responsible for any costs associated with us implementing those procedures. You will also be liable to pay us interest from the time the invoice becomes overdue to the time we receive payment.
YOUR RIGHTS ABOUT LEGAL COSTS
The following avenues are available to you if you are not happy with this invoice:
  • Requesting an itemised invoice;
  • Discussing your concerns with us;
  • Having our costs adjudicated;
  • Applying to set aside our costs agreement;
  • Making a complaint to the Legal Profession Conduct
Commissioner or Office of the Legal Services Commissioner (if you believe there has been overcharging) There may be other avenues available in your State or Territory (such as mediation). For more information about your rights, please read the fact sheet titled Your Right to Challenge Legal Costs. CEASING TO CONTINUE TO ACT
If you have not settled this account within 30 days of its receipt, we will cease to continue to act for you in relation to any current matters on which we have been instructed. We will not recommence to act for you in relation to any such matters, until we have received full payment of this invoice.
